About Pinnacle

About Pinnacle

Pinnacle University, located between Chennai and Bangalore in the heart of India's Silicon Valley, is one of the world's leading teaching and research universities. Since its opening in 1891, Pinnacle has been dedicated to finding solutions to big challenges and to preparing students for leadership in a complex world.
